​Directions
We are located at Biosciences Research Laboratory, on the Ahmedabad University south campus in the Ahmedabad City district of Ahmedabad, Gujarat. We are a two minute walk from University Road/Ring Road and Shree Pannalal Patel Marg. One minute walk from university bus stop at the university road.
Trains, cabs and buses:
We are a twenty-one minute car drive from the Ahmedabad Junction train station (via Kasturba Gandhi Road). If visiting from center city, the 40/3, 47, 50, 56/1, 103, and 144 bus lines run west and stop at university stop on university road. Our Lab is one minute walk from there.
